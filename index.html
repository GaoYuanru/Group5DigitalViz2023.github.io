History of Manchester

Revolution, Destruction, and Rebirth.

1760s~1840s

Mancester become the first industrial city in the world. The population has tripled during the first half of the century.

1940

Manchester was hit by two nights of air raids, referred to as the 'Christmas Blitz'. Commercial centres and residential areas were most badly affected by air raids.

1996

The IRA detonated a 1,500-kilogram lorry bomb on Corporation Street in the centre of Manchester.
