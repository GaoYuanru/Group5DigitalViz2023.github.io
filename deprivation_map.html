The Index of Multiple Deprivation (IMD) is an overall relative measure of deprivation constructed by combining seven domains of deprivation according to their respective weights.

This choropleth map shows the change in GM's deprivation score from 2010 to 2019.

Firstly, at the local authority level, the change in average score for each borough is shown. Where blue represents a decrease in deprivation score and red represents an increase in score. The page is set up with different buttons to control the viewing of the six different deprivation domains, and as we want to show the changes that urban renewal has brought to the city, the relative change data is used here. When the mouse is hovered over, the index of multiple deprivation data can be viewed for two different years.
